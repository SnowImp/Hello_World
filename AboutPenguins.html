Penguins - the bird that flies underwater!

Penguins are flightless birds that live in the artic and antartic regions of the world. These birds are covered by think feathers that allow them to have a completely water proof covering when they swim through the water.

Emporer Penguin

The largest penguins, 122 cm (48 in) tall.

Attributes

  1. Antarctica
  2. Orange ear patches on sides of head
  3. Taller than other penguin species

Emperor Penguin
